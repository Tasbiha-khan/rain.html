Rainwater Harvesting Techniques

Rooftop Rainwater Harvesting System

Rooftop rainwater harvesting is a technique in which rainwater falling on the roof of a building is collected, filtered, and stored for future use.This method is commonly used in residential and commercial buildings because it is simple,and cost-effective rainwater falling on the rooftop of a building is collected through gutters and conveyance pipes and then passed through a treatment system to remove impurities. After filtration, the water is stored in a subsurface storage tank where it is kept safe for future use.
